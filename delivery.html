Delivery Information

We deliver every day between 11:00 and 18:00. Orders can be made by phone at 056 123 456.

Delivery is free in the following neighbourhoods: Center, Lazur, Zornitsa, Izgrev, Slaveykov.

For Sarafovo, Kraimorie and Meden Rudnik, a delivery fee of 5.00 BGN applies.
